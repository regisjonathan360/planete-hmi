# Product

**Planète HMI** is a cosmic/space-themed web platform built around artists and their content.

## What it is

A visual, immersive experience where artists are presented within themed "districts" of a planet. The look and feel leans heavily on a space aesthetic: planets, nebulas, stars, and cosmic glow effects layered behind artist banners, covers, and profiles.

## Key concepts

- **Artists** — creators featured on the platform, each with banner and square imagery.
- **Covers** — artwork associated with artist content (banner and square formats).
- **Districts** — themed sections/zones of the planet used to organize or group content.
- **Immersive backgrounds** — layered cosmic visuals (nebula, stars, planet foreground) that define the brand experience across desktop and mobile.

## Status

Early stage. The repository currently contains only brand assets and imagery; application source code has not yet been added.

## Conventions

- Primary project language is **French** (UI copy, comments, config descriptions). Keep user-facing text in French unless asked otherwise.
- Responsive by default: assets are provided in dedicated desktop and mobile variants.
- Prefer `.webp` for delivered imagery where possible; `.png`/`.svg` originals are kept alongside.
